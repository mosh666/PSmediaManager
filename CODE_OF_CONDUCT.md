# Code of Conduct

PSmediaManager adopts the spirit of the Contributor Covenant to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Summary

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Focus discussions on technical merit.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

Report concerns to the maintainer (contact to be defined). Actions may include warnings or, for severe violations, contribution restrictions.
